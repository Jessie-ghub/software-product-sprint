The Pig Game

The game of Pig is a simple dice game where two players race to reach 100 points.

On each turn, the current player repeatedly rolls a die until a "1" (score nothing) is rolled or the player decides to "hold" (turn total is added to their score)

The first player to score 100 or more points wins.
